Past Events

My latest work was exhibited as a part of a joint show called

'HIDDEN'

A 3-day exhibition in Shoreditch Town Hall’s basement, a Grade II-listed heritage building in Central London.

'HIDDEN' was an exhibition that weaved together two introspective projects ('Brainwrapped' & 'Can you see yourself ?') by dyscalculic artist Alexa Raisbeck and myself.

Date:

28th - 30th June 2011

Location:

Shoreditch Town Hall

Shoreditch Town Hall’s basement is a combination of underground chambers and has a very strong character with many uneven surfaces and low light areas. It has a dungeon/cellar-like atmosphere with traces of life from the past, with blind underground windows and doors, cabinets and fireplaces, heavy iron ovens and pipes.

It was perfect for a show that meant to offer the experience of wondering in and exploring your own mind.

Will 'HIDDEN' return to Shoreditch?
