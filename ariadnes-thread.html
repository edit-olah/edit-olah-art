Ariadne's thread

2011: Wool and wire - Installation

According to a Greek myth Ariadne aided her half-brother Theseus in overcoming the Minotaur by giving him a ball of woollen thread to navigate the Cretan labyrinth allowing him to retrace his path after killing the "Bull of Minos" - a monster (or a god?).

The term, Ariadneâ€™s thread, is also a term used to describe an algorithm, the solving of a problem with multiple apparent means of proceeding. The method is used to trace a series of found truths in a contingent. The process can take the form of a mental record, a physical marking, or a philosophical debate.
